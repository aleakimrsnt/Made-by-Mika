UX/UI Projects

KASAMA – Student Commute Companion
Application Pitch Project

React Native, Firebase

A conceptual mobile application designed to assist students in managing their daily commutes with greater ease and reliability. The UI design, created in Figma, emphasizes clarity, safety, and accessibility, addressing common challenges faced during travel. Presented as a prototype and project proposal, it showcases thoughtful problem-solving and user-centered design.

iSeeker – Invasive Crop and Pest Identification App

Canva

A mobile interface concept that supports farmers in identifying invasive plants and pests through image recognition. The UI, designed with soft green tones and a clean layout, reflects its agricultural focus while keeping the experience simple, clear, and easy to navigate. It highlights usability and accessibility, ensuring that essential information is delivered in a straightforward and visually engaging way.

BlossomCart – M-Commerce Flower Shop

Canva, Figma

It is a vibrant e-commerce interface concept designed for a flower shop. The UI features a warm gradient background with soft pink and peach tones, emphasizing an inviting and cheerful shopping experience. It showcases clean layouts for product browsing, simple login and checkout flows, and a user-friendly profile section, highlighting clarity, accessibility, and aesthetic appeal.
